# Business: 16x NeoPixel Display

## Revenue Model
Sell as a complete plug-and-play programmable display unit with enclosure and web UI. Secondary: sell firmware/software license for DIY builders.

## Target Market
- Art installations and interactive displays
- Retail / hospitality ambiance displays
- Maker / hobbyist community (kit form)
- Event signage (custom animations, scrolling text)

## Pricing Strategy
- Unit price: $200–300 (assembled)
- Kit price: $120–150
- Margin: ~60–70%

## Competitive Advantage
- ESP32 + WiFi + OTA built-in — remotely updatable
- Custom animation API — easy to drive from any app or browser
- Open architecture for maker customization

## Go-to-Market
- Etsy / Tindie for initial sales to maker market
- Instagram / TikTok demo content (animations, Game of Life, text scroll)
- Direct approach to local retail/hospitality once v1.0 is stable
